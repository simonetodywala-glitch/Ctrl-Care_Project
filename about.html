About Ctrl+Care

The story of two friends, a passion for helping others, and a mission to make artificial intelligence understandable for everyone.

Our Story

It started as a simple idea between two high school sophomores: Simone Todywala and Jhanvi Rapolou. They noticed something important — while artificial intelligence was changing everything, information about it felt meant only for a select few.

👫

High Schoolers on a Mission

Simone, a sophomore, felt it's important that more people are aware of the future of AI. Jhanvi, another sophomore, felt that it shouldn't matter whether you have a degree or some professional background as a computer scientist.

Together, they decided to create Ctrl+Care — a resource that's warm, friendly, and truly accessible. No jargon without explanation. No assumptions about prior knowledge. Just clear, caring instruction that met people where they were.

We're high school students looking to make a difference in our community, and we believe AI literacy should be for everyone.

Our Mission

Making AI make sense — for everyone.

We're two high school sophomores who believe that artificial intelligence shouldn't be the domain of a tech elite. We believe that every person — no matter their age, education, or background — deserves to understand the tools that are shaping their world. Ctrl+Care is our promise to make that a reality, one friendly explanation at a time.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do at Ctrl+Care.

📖

Learn

We believe in learning that works for you, at your pace, on your terms. Complex ideas can be made clear when we take the time to explain them right.

💝

Care

We approach every interaction with warmth and patience. You don't need to be perfect to learn about AI — you just need to be willing to explore with us.

🌈

Include

AI literacy should be for everyone. We're committed to reaching learners of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds, and ensuring no one is left behind.
